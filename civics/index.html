InSeed Civics

Helping governments, regulators, and public-facing organisations evolve in a hybrid human + AI world.

InSeed works with leaders who feel the ground shifting under their institutions: AI is changing not only tools, but the very shape of obligations to citizens, employees, and future generations.

If you work in government or public institutions, this is likely the most relevant place to start.

Why Civics now?

Most “AI transformation” programmes start from tools and automation. InSeed starts from civic obligations: what it means to be a steward of public trust when intelligence is no longer only human.

  • Protecting the integrity of democratic and civic institutions
  • Making AI-enabled services legible and accountable to citizens
  • Designing guardrails that are owned at the edge, not just in the platform
  • Creating space for experimentation without sacrificing trust

Who we work with

InSeed’s Civics work is designed for:

  • Senior civil servants and public sector executives
  • Regulators and policy teams facing AI-driven change
  • Large enterprises with significant civic and regulatory exposure
  • Civic innovators, foundations, and coalitions building new models

How we work

We do not start from templates or pre-baked answers. We start from your actual institutions, people, and constraints, and we work forward with you in a series of design waves.

  • Short, high-signal advisory sprints with leadership teams
  • Joint design of guardrails, governance, and operating principles
  • Roadmaps for “legacy to steward” transformations
  • Optional collaboration with the open-source CoCivium ecosystem

Our incentives are transparent. InSeed exists to accelerate healthy evolution of civic and economic systems, not to maximise billable hours.
