Case studies from the InSeed constellation

InSeed works inside a small constellation of brands and labs that explore steward-minded strategy, governance and organisational change in the AI era. Many of these are internal experiments, but they behave like real clients: they have boards, stakeholders, risks and deadlines.

OpenName

Identity and reputation experiments for a hybrid society.

OpenName explores how handles, identity and privileges should work when humans and AIs share public spaces. InSeed designs the governance and trust scaffolding: how names are issued, how trust accumulates and how misuse is gently cooled without public shaming.

  • Focus: identity, trust, safety.
  • InSeed work: steward IDs, halo and merit models, escalation paths.

Entment

Entertainment that trains civic reflexes instead of eroding them.

Entment treats stories and games as training grounds for future citizens. InSeed helps rewire incentives so success is measured in long-term trust and stewardship, not just clicks and short-term engagement.

  • Focus: narrative, incentives, civic reflexes.
  • InSeed work: product and story framing, metrics, guardrails for AI-assisted content.

StartupsBoard

AI-literate governance for founders and boards.

StartupsBoard is a lab for how young companies should update board practices in an AI-heavy world. InSeed designs playbooks, briefings and training formats so directors can see the full risk and opportunity landscape without being paralysed by jargon.

  • Focus: governance, risk, board education.
  • InSeed work: board packs, decision frameworks, AI-literate reporting formats.

BeAxa

Personal agency and transformation in an AI age.

BeAxa focuses on individual leaders and teams who want to become better versions of themselves with AI as an ally, not a threat. InSeed shapes programmes that move people from overwhelm into calm, steward-minded experimentation.

  • Focus: leadership, coaching, personal change.
  • InSeed work: journey design, cohort formats, feedback and reflection loops.

WhyHead

Thinking about thinking for decision-makers.

WhyHead is about meta-cognition for executives: how do we notice our blind spots, update our mental models and make better calls under uncertainty. InSeed provides diagnostics, workshop formats and follow-up routines that help organisations actually use these insights.

  • Focus: decision quality, cognitive hygiene.
  • InSeed work: diagnostics, facilitator guides, integration into normal ops.
