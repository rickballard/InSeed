About InSeed

We’re a boutique business strategy and project-management consultancy. We help leadership teams pivot around better systems, processes, and self-governance: pairing interim executives with a networked bench of specialists (human+AI) so strategy survives contact with reality.

What we do

  • CoAudit (Week 0): snapshot of product/process and risks. Output: 30-60-90 plan & scoreboard.
  • CoFlow: weekly execution engine that ships changes and instruments the work.
  • CoMirror (Day seminar): principles, bets, constraints, and 90-day commitments.
  • TOS-AI Steward: governance guardrails for AI-accelerated delivery.

How we work

  • Visible outcomes: shipped deltas on a scoreboard, not slideware.
  • Governance-first: principles & constraints coupled to weekly execution.
  • No lock-in: we standardize the on-ramp so your team can run it without us.
  • Transparent rates: see Billing & engagement (Week-0 CoAudit is free).

InSeed History

Since 1990. InSeed began in Denver, Colorado, as a boutique consultancy supporting venture-backed global launches in the datacoms sector. We’ve rarely grown past 20 staff: by design: staying a strategy-first firm with strong technology expertise and a track record in partnerships/ecosystems, systems integration, market launches, and product/service prototyping, testing, and verification.

That bias toward practical outcomes also enabled occasional angel investments in “moonshots,” a few of which became billion-dollar unicorns. The founder later stepped back for ~20 years to study while continuing angel investing (especially Canadian software teams), adding technology-heavy degrees and focusing on business-vision disciplines.

Today: InSeed operates as both (a) an angel/venture partner focused on CoCivium and adjacent ventures, and (b) a partner association of similarly senior, similarly motivated organization builders. CoCivium is our umbrella for civic-scale, human-centered digital infrastructure and coordination tools: aimed at healthier markets, institutions, and communities.

Independence & exemplar policy

  • Independent and vendor-agnostic: we take no reseller or referral fees. We design multi-vendor strategies to avoid single-AI bias and lock-in.
  • Temporary by design: our role is oversight and enablement so your team owns it. We aim to be not needed quickly.
  • One exemplar per vertical: we seek a single best-in-class client in each industry to deepen practice and reduce conflicts. We cross-pollinate best practices across sectors in support of CoCivium.
