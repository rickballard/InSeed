History & perspective

We started InSeed to help CEOs adapt faster than vendor roadmaps. In the AI era, advantage compounds when you assemble capabilities in your own repos, ship weekly evidence with real usage, and avoid getting boxed into a product‚Äôs UI or pricing.

Principles we run on

  • Assemble > Buy: Use vendors for commodities; keep differentiators (prompts, flows, tools, evals) in your repos.
  • CoFlow cadence: one small capability per week; monthly pivots with leadership.
  • Guardrails are deliverables: TOS-AI gates, data boundaries, incident learning baked into each release.
  • Independent systems integration: multi-vendor adapters; exit paths written down.
  • Evidence over claims: scoreboards that track lead time, adoption, rework, ‚Äúdecisions improved‚Äù.

Your business model faces enough threats, AI should not be one of them.

InSeed provides short-term Executives and Board advisors, typically focusing on technology realignment for service businesses where AI has already reshaped market demand and competitor offerings. As automation scales (think of each employee managing a hundred virtual subordinates), the role of humans must move up the value chain: less grind, more vision. Our clients are usually large organizations (enterprises, governments and non-profits) which require operational guardrails against data-exposure, sourcing-bias or vendor lock-in, measurable outcomes to verify market alignment and sustainability, and seasoned strategists to keep the business vision "congruent" with hybrid workflow (human+AI).

We help you create the plan, and we support delivery in an advisory capacity, supporting but not replacing your project management offices. We help to define systems requirements, solution briefs, RFPs, and we benchmark offerings against global best practices.
