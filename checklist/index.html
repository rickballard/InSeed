Engagement

Executive Checklist

Answer a few quick Yes/No questions. Tips appear as you go. Privacy.

Q1. Are vendor contracts limiting your ability to move data between systems?
Tip: Map “data egress” clauses per vendor; negotiate a standing export job to S3/Blob.
Q2. Do you rely on manual spreadsheet joins for weekly metrics?
Tip: Stand up a tiny ELT (one table) into a governed store; add one metric a week.
Q3. Is there tension between “AI pilots” and production teams?
Tip: Create a one-page “TOS-AI” runway: intake → sandbox → pilot → guardrails → service.
