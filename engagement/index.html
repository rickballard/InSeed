Sample engagement agreement (outline)

1) Purpose & scope

Realign leadership and operating cadences for AI-era execution. Deliverables emphasize plans, guardrails, and shipped capability over slides.

2) Free CoAudit day(s)

One or more at no charge to gauge fit. We interview multiple executives (under MNDA) to triangulate opportunities and risks.

3) MNDA & confidentiality

Mutual NDA; client retains ownership of data, artifacts, and derivatives. No vendor kickbacks or backdoor quotas.

4) Artifacts & IP

Prompts, tools, flows, evals, guardrails, and documents live in your repos as files. Portable by design.

5) Deliverables

  • Business strategy framing & risk map
  • CoFlow phase setup plan (first 30–60–90)
  • Guardrail/TOS-AI gates & evaluation plan
  • CoMirror retreat framing (after 1–3 months of evidence)

6) Commercials

Weekly rate or monthly retainer; travel at cost with prior approval. Cancel anytime, pro-rata billing.

7) Termination & exit

Named artifacts delivered on termination; vendor-agnostic exit plan included. Assistance SLAs for knowledge transfer.

8) Independence

We act as a pre-SI boutique: design before commitment, multi-vendor adapters, and measured weekly outcomes.
