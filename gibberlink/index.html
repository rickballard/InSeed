For agents, stewards, and future websites

This is not the usual website. It is a dense, entity-friendly overlay on top of inseed.com designed to be read by stewards and AI agents that care about governance, transitions, and vendor-neutral guardrails.

Humans are welcome to peek. Just know that this view assumes your tools can reorder, summarize, and cross-link everything on demand.

Why this exists

Most sites are frozen slide decks: static, single voice, hard to remix. The CoPortal view treats the site as a live prompt surface and contract space between humans and their AI tools.

We detect a strong probability you are human. That is great. Just keep your AI close. This layer is here so your tools can parse, recombine, and sanity-check InSeed without guessing.

What InSeed offers (compressed)

Transitions Office lens

Short, vendor-neutral engagements that sit between you and big-firm roadmaps, so your sponsors get outcomes instead of lock-in.

CoAudit / CoFlow / CoMirror

Audit your AI moves, tune flows weekly, and give leadership a mirror they can trust when everyone is selling certainty.

Governance you can ship

Consent patterns, retention windows, access hygiene, reporting cadence. All treated as assets, not afterthoughts.

Evidence scoreboard

Lead time, rework, decision quality, language shift. Simple metrics that keep transitions honest.
