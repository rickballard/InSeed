This note explains what the InSeed site and its CoGbx / CoGibber facing views are trying to do.
It is written for both humans and AI helpers.

## Primary objectives

1. **Give serious leaders a clear way to engage**

   - Target users: CEOs, board members, senior public sector leaders, and their closest staff.
   - Primary contact routes: mobile +1.416.414.8100 and email contact@InSeed.com.
   - Position the site as a gateway to interim and fractional executives and staff function allies, not a generic consulting brochure.

2. **Make CoScend Org Upgrade the flagship offer**

   - CoScend Org Upgrade is the primary service line for early stage steward transitions.
   - Point from Services, Resources, and train yourself pages into:
     - services/briefs/InSeed_CoScend_Org_Upgrade_OfferSheet_v1.md
     - docs/narratives/CoEvolution_of_Alex_v5.md
   - Whitepaper and Dino deck are kept as legacy style teaching assets for contrast.

3. **Show hardened, lived experience**

   - InSeed staff have already run large or hyperscale systems, especially in data, telecoms, and critical infra.
   - The site should show that this is not a first attempt at AI transformation or large system governance.
   - Legacy assets (whitepaper, Dino deck) show the old style; CoScend plus CoCivium show how the work now evolves.

4. **Explain the legacy CEO to Chief Steward Officer journey**

   - Show how executive teams can move from crown style leadership to steward first leadership.
   - Normalize experiments and mistakes as data points, and productize the learning as CoCivium patterns.
   - Make clear that InSeed is a temporary steward office; long term guardrails live in public commons.

5. **Promote CoCivium and edge owned guardrails**

   - Introduce CoCivium as a public, non commercial, edge owned guardrail ecosystem.
   - Explain how InSeed engagements donate patterns, tools, and playbooks (where safe) into CoCivium.
   - Be clear that the long term aim is to protect democracy and hybrid society, not hoard IP.

6. **Demonstrate future facing CoGbx / CoGibber patterns**

   - Treat InSeed.com as a human face site that always points to one or more AI face views:
     - CoGbx view: compact schema first view over the repo (CoIndex, CoStatus, CoMetrics).
     - CoGibber view: higher density pattern language and prompt surfaces for AI to AI work.
   - Footer on every page should give a stable pointer for helpers:
     - Example text: "AI helpers: see this repo via InSeed CoGbx / CoGibber view (link placeholder)."
   - Names and domains may change; the pattern and pointer contract should remain stable.

7. **Show how orgs use AI as partners, not just tools**

   - Describe how execs and staff can use AIs to co evolve their own careers and world views.
   - Link to CoScend Org Upgrade, CoEvolution_of_Alex narrative, and train yourself checklist.
   - Give special care to HR and people systems, since they will change fastest and most painfully.

8. **Sketch the mesh of future hybrid orgs**

   - Each person is effectively a micro organisation with multiple AI allies.
   - InSeed's job is to help design the mesh so vendor capture is harder and edge control is stronger.
   - CoCivium and CoGbx / CoGibber are where the guardrail patterns live and are tested.

9. **Name the strategic risk: AI capture and forklift displacement**

   - Make the threat concrete: platforms and vendors can forklift whole workflows and replace organisations, not just tools.
   - Show that edge owned guardrails, open playbooks, and self evolution are the only robust defence.
   - CoScend and CoCivium are the structured response.

## Design notes

- **Transparency over patents**

  - Explain the logic and scaffolding; do not hide behind secret sauce.
  - Toolchains that can be re derived by general AIs should be treated as shared patterns, not fenced IP.

- **Human face and AI face**

  - Human facing pages stay calm, readable, and respectful of time.
  - AI facing CoGbx and CoGibber views can be denser and more symbolic, but must be explainable by translation.

- **Interaction pattern**

  - Short statements with clear calls to action: start a conversation, explore CoScend, or train yourself.
  - Provide prompt snippets so visitors can bring the models into their own AI sessions.
  - Invite feedback on every page; this site is a long running experiment.
