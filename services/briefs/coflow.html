CoFlow (delivery oversight & sourcing) — sample engagement brief

Client work is confidential. These briefs are composites: anonymized, data-stripped, and generalized so the approach is relevant across industries.

Context

Enterprise opted to keep delivery with vendors. The board asked for independent oversight that prevents single-AI bias and vendor lock-in while keeping strategy congruent with implementation.

What InSeed did

  • Designed RFP briefs and sourcing strategy with multi-vendor lanes and exit ramps.
  • Built an evaluation matrix aligned to guardrails, data provenance, and interoperability.
  • Benchmarked proposals against global best practice. Ran evidence reviews every week.
  • Prototyped target workflows, policies, and controls. Updated SOPs and training plans.

Deliverables

  • RFP briefs and vendor evaluation matrix. Decision log and architecture notes.
  • Risk and compliance checks: leakage, lock-in, red-team, and audit trail.
  • Scoreboard updates with shipped evidence from vendors. Go/hold recommendations.

Timeline & team

12-week cadence. One InSeed lead as advisor, plus client owners. Vendor teams deliver; InSeed monitors evidence and alignment.
