Client work is confidential. These briefs are composites: anonymized, data-stripped, and generalized so the approach is relevant across industries.

Context

Large enterprise preparing a pivot into an AI-enabled marketplace. Leadership wanted a fast scan of processes, risks, and opportunity to build a 30-60-90 plan with a visible scoreboard.

What InSeed did

  • Mapped priority workflows and systems. Captured guardrails for data, compliance, and brand.
  • Identified 3–5 bet lines with measurable outcomes and owners.
  • Established the 30-60-90 scoreboard and evidence cadence. Set principles, constraints, and red lines.

Deliverables

  • Scoreboard: outcomes, metrics, owners, weekly evidence plan.
  • Risk register: leakage, vendor lock-in, model drift, and mitigations.
  • On-ramp plan: enablement, SOP updates, comms, and change map.

Timeline & team

Week 0. One InSeed lead plus 2–3 stakeholders per function for interviews and evidence gathering.
