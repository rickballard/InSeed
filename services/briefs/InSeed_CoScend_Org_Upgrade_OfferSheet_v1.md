## 1. Who this is for

This service is for organizations that:

- Feel the pressure of AI, remote work and new expectations  
- Know the old pyramid model is creaking  
- Do not want to blow themselves up with a reckless "flat org" experiment  

Typical buyers:

- Chief Executive Officer or equivalent Steward  
- Board members who take long term stewardship seriously  
- Heads of Strategy or Transformation  
- Forward looking HR or People leaders who are not afraid of change  

---

## 2. Problem in plain language

Legacy organizations are built like pyramids.

They depend on:

- Titles and hierarchy to coordinate work  
- Annual planning and slow feedback loops  
- People like Alex being rewarded mainly for "doing their job"  

AI, hybrid work and global competition are making that model brittle.

Common symptoms:

- Endless meetings that do not change real world outcomes  
- Silent anxiety among process thinkers and technical staff  
- "Innovation" teams that are separated from the real work  
- Strong talent leaving to join smaller, more agile companies  

Most executives know something is wrong.  
They do not always have words for what needs to change.

---

## 3. What CoScend Org Upgrade does

CoScend Org Upgrade is a guided transformation that:

1. Maps your current organization in terms of **flows** instead of boxes  
2. Identifies where **CoCells** and **CoConductors** could replace brittle chains of command  
3. Re frames key roles (including people like Alex) around **strength first titles**  
4. Installs a light but real **Stewardship circle** that can make and hold long term intent  
5. Puts in place simple **guardrails and accords** so that people and AI can work together safely  

This is not a rebrand exercise.  
It is a practical redesign of how work actually gets done.

---

## 4. Outcomes you can expect

By the end of a CoScend Org Upgrade, you can expect:

- A clear picture of your current org as flows, not just as an org chart  
- A first ring of CoCells with named Stewards and CoConductors  
- At least one high leverage area (often a product or service line)  
  operating under the new pattern  
- Updated titles and role descriptions for key process thinkers  
  so that their value is visible and future aligned  
- A first version of a **CoSteward / Contributor Accord**  
  tailored to your organization  

Soft outcomes:

- Reduced anxiety among high potential staff who see a real future  
- A more honest board conversation about AI, risk and opportunity  
- A shared language for "how we evolve" rather than "who reports to whom"

## 5. How the work runs (phases)

This is a template. Real projects will be sized to the client.

### Phase 1 – Org Scan and Sanity+Orgs Review (2 to 3 weeks)

- Short discovery interviews with executives, HR and process thinkers  
- Mapping of critical flows and pain points  
- Sanity+Orgs lens applied:
  - Where are we fooling ourselves?  
  - Where are we brittle?  
  - Where are people like Alex being wasted or pushed out?  

Deliverables:

- Org as flows map (simple, human readable)  
- Sanity+Orgs risk and opportunity summary  
- Initial list of candidate CoCells and CoConductors  

### Phase 2 – CoNeural Org Design Sprint (2 to 3 weeks)

- Collaborative sessions to design:
  - Steward circle for long term intent  
  - First wave of CoCells  
  - CoConductor roles and interactions  
- Early work on strength first titles for key staff  
- Early draft of a CoSteward / Contributor Accord  

Deliverables:

- CoNeural org blueprint (v1)  
- CoCell map and role sketches  
- Draft Accord text (internal only at this stage)  

### Phase 3 – Pilot and Accord (4 to 6 weeks)

- Launch of the first CoCells in a narrow but meaningful area  
- Coaching for Stewards, CoConductors and staff  
- Refinement and negotiation of the Accord so it fits culture and law  
- Simple metrics for:
  - delivery speed,  
  - employee signal and sentiment,  
  - risk and guardrail health  

Deliverables:

- Pilot report and metrics  
- CoSteward / Contributor Accord (v1.0) ready for wider adoption  
- Clear recommendations for scaling or adjusting the pattern
