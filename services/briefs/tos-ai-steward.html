TOS-AI steward — sample engagement brief

Client work is confidential. These briefs are composites: anonymized, data-stripped, and generalized so the approach is relevant across industries.

Context

Organization needed guardrails so hybrid workflows remain safe and auditable while moving fast.

What InSeed did

  • Drafted AI principles, risk appetite, and roles. Named ultimate accountability.
  • Set policy on data access, provenance, and third-party model use.
  • Defined controls for leakage, lock-in, and model drift. Scheduled red-team exercises.

Deliverables

  • Policy pack and SOP updates. Incident playbook and audit trail design.
  • Scorecard for incidents, mitigations, and time-to-contain.

Timeline & team

4–6 weeks to stand up. Ongoing light-touch reviews as part of CoFlow or CoMirror.
