CoMirror (day seminar): sample engagement brief

Client work is confidential. These briefs are composites: anonymized, data-stripped, and generalized so the approach is relevant across industries.

Context

Leadership required periodic alignment so delivery stays tied to principle, constraints, and the scoreboard.

What InSeed did

  • Facilitated a day session to reset principles, select bets, and confirm constraints.
  • Challenged evidence and refreshed the 90-day plan.
  • Agreed stop-start-continue list and owner accountability.

Deliverables

  • Updated 90-day scoreboard with owners and dates.
  • Decisions, risks, and comms pack for the wider org.

Timeline & team

One day each quarter. Executives plus key owners. InSeed facilitates and records outcomes.
