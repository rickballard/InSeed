CoFlow

A small, steady flow of high-value changes—prioritized with your execs, shipped weekly, guarded for safety, and measured.

What you get

  • Weekly shipped slice in production or pilot
  • Runway: intake → sandbox → guarded pilot → service owner
  • Guardrails (TOS-AI) and change notes
  • Simple KPI string: lead time, usage, decisions improved

Engagement shape

  • Starter (4–6 weeks): two workflows, weekly ship cadence.
  • Steady (quarter): one small capability per week; monthly review.

Is this for us?

Best when you have eager teams and a few nagging cross-system workflows. Not a fit for “big-bang” platform rewrites.
