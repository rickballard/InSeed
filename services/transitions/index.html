What this pilot is for

We help leadership teams navigate handovers with less friction and more measurable buy-in. This 6‚Äì8 week pilot is designed to prove the pattern in one setting, with clear evidence of what works and where governance or workflows need tightening.

How the pilot works

  • Co-design a transition scenario with your Transition or Transformation Office.
  • Run a small number of structured sessions with a "listener in the corner" capturing discussion signals.
  • Distinguish process-building habits from purely tribal ones.
  • Return debriefs, playbooks, and governance templates your teams can reuse.

Governance and privacy

  • Explicit consent patterns for participants.
  • Clear retention windows for recordings and notes.
  • Role hygiene: who can see what, and when.
  • Reporting cadence that keeps sponsors informed without overexposing individuals.

What you get at the end

  • A repeatable transition exercise and facilitator kit.
  • Signals and metrics: engagement, language shift, follow-ups, time-to-competency.
  • Governance assets (consent, retention, access control) reusable across clients or business units.
  • A simple scoreboard format that can be extended to Multi-site and Program levels.
