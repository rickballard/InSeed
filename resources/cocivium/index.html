CoCivium

CoCivium is an independent not-for-profit initiative to develop open-source practices and commons for a hybrid society of humans and AI. It is set up to outlast any single company or era.

  • Independent: governed outside InSeed
  • Open-source: methods, artifacts, and knowledge are public
  • Not designed for financial return: impact over IRR
  • InSeed’s role: we support and invest in the early stages, but our influence is temporary by design
