Legacy vs Steward — Exec Summary (Sample)

Intent: a quick contrast for busy CEOs; the full whitepaper is the canonical, downloadable reference.

What changes

CEO/L

  • Buys solution, then implements
  • Roadmap follows vendor cadence
  • Artifacts trapped in a UI/DB
  • Evidence = slideware + anecdotes
  • Big SI/consulting lead the design

Steward

  • Assembles differentiators in own repos
  • Ships a usable slice weekly, pivots monthly
  • File-based prompts/flows/evals are portable
  • Evidence = shared scoreboard & guardrails
  • Vendor-neutral pre-SI boutique sets the pattern

How to start

  • Pick 2 workflows; ship 1 slice this week
  • Track adoption, lead-time, rework %, decisions improved
  • Install TxO Steward gates; capture incident “efficiency wisdom”
