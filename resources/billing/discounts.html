Discount policy & grants

We prioritize CoCivium-aligned work. When the mission is right, we discount deeply — sometimes to pro bono — and may even underwrite costs.

Our stance

We exist to help leaders build safer, more capable civic and commercial systems. That includes pro bono work when the public-good impact is clear and the team is ready to turn evidence into outcomes.

  • CoCivium focus: projects that augment education, research, healthcare, resilience, open knowledge, and civic infrastructure.
  • Ethics first: no surveillance capitalism, predatory finance, or disinformation work.
  • Evidence cadence: discounts go farther when teams ship weekly slices and share a scoreboard.

Who qualifies?

  • Registered charities / 501(c)(3) / UK charities / B-Corps with civic outcomes
  • Public interest labs, universities & hospitals, open-source foundations
  • Early-stage ventures with clear public-good angle (case-by-case)

Discount tiers

TierTypical reductionNotes
Pro bono / grant70–100%When the cause is acute and our leverage is high. We may also underwrite expenses, resulting in negative cash flow on our side.
Charity / B-Corp50–70%For well-run orgs with strong stewardship; we prefer matching funds or co-sponsors when available.
Civic-aligned startup20–40%Sliding scale; equity or revenue-share is possible if appropriate.

Transparency: Discounts apply to our published sticker rates. We reserve the right to cap volume or pause when capacity is tight.

Example impact: Ukraine aid

We have supported humanitarian aid to Ukraine with partners including Dogs’n’Homes Rescue (UK), moving hundreds of tonnes of supplies via European charity logistics. This reflects our belief that leadership, portability, and weekly evidence can save real lives.

How to request support

  1. Start with a Week-0 CoAudit (free) so we can map your workflows, risks, and scoreboard.
  2. Describe the public-good outcome and who benefits (be concrete).
  3. Name one workflow we can make usable within 1–2 weeks.
  4. Confirm data guardrails and a decision-maker who can meet weekly.

Publicity & governance

  • We default to quiet delivery; case studies require your approval.
  • No conflicts of interest; we avoid vendor kickbacks or exclusivity.
  • Artifact portability is mandatory: you keep prompts, tools, flows, and evals.
