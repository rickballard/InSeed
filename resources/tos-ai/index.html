TOS-AI Steward

Hero placeholder

TOS-AI stands for Transition Office Steward with AI specialties. It is a temporary executive role that supports or stands up a Transition Office during a high-stakes pivot.

  • Guardrails: protects scope, decision rights, and cultural non-negotiables
  • Board-safe: clear metrics and auditable methods
  • Vendor-neutral: aligns external firms to your frame, not the other way round
  • Short duration: InSeed supplies interim leaders; we do not build dependency
