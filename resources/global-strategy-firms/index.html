CEO Survival Guide (Serious)

How to harness global strategy firms without losing control.

How they work

  • Arrive with a Board mandate, not a CEO mandate
  • Deploy scale-optimized playbooks
  • Replace executive decision rights with “program governance”

Risks if unconstrained

  • CEO sidelined
  • Executives reduced to implementers
  • Inflated timelines and deliverables
  • Accountability lands with you after they exit

The fix (what InSeed prepares)

  • Frame scope and success metrics before they arrive
  • Define decision rights and non-negotiables
  • Repackage executive value so your leaders remain indispensable
  • Install a TOS-AI Steward to manage the onramp and keep consultants aligned
